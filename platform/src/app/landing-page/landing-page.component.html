Who are we

we are a non profit organization who aims to revolutionize the educational sector by rethinking our daily struggles and putting our creative skills into action to solve these problems. We aim to spread this positive attitude across people and especially youth conclusion: We, as young ambassadors of change, as citizens who believe that digital literacy is a human right and the new universal language is the language of technology, believe that our project will represent a great revolutionization to the educational sector internationally, but also more specifically on a nationwide level, a project made by passionate teens who went through the system, faced problems, and figured that the only way to fix these issues is by taking the initiative and doing it ourselves, a project made by students for students and - hopefully - for a brighter Tunisia. Thank you.

What we do

Innovation, Discipline, Safety … What more words describe what every learner needs? It’s our mission to enable every child to keep them safe and reach their goals and design everything they need for their education. so we decide to help the administration of every school to manage them easily and provide safety for them to become the leaders of the future.
